SCL: P3.2
SDA: P3.0
R4 (lower byte): Tx Buffer
R5 (lower byte): Rx Buffer
R15 reserved for Tx/Rx bit counting

----------- tx_byte pseudocode ------------
R4: xxxx xxxx 0101 0101
	      ^
	     MSb

--> MSb bit mask = 0x80

bit.b 0x80, R4
jz send_zero

send_one
	SDA high
	jmp tx_scl

send_zero
	SDA low
	jmp tx_scl

tx_scl:
	setup_delay
	SCL high
	clock_high_delay
	SCL low
	hold_delay

next_bit
	rlc R4
	dec.w R15
	jnz tx_loop

	mov.w #9d, R15
	pop R4
	


START:
- Set SCL and SDA as outputs
- Send SDA low
- Delay minimum 4 us (start hold)
- Send SCL low

SEND Address:
- Set A6 on SDA
- Delay minimum 250 ns (data setup) 
- Send SCL high
- Delay minimum 5 us (clock hold)
- Send SCL low
- Delay minimum 5 us (data hold)
- ... A5, A4, A3, A2, A1, A0, R/W ...
- Set SDA port to input
- Delay minimum 250 ns (ACK setup)
- Send SCL high
- Delay minimum 5 us (clock hold)
- Send SCL low
