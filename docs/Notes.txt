SCL: P3.2
SDA: P3.0

START:
- Send SDA low
- Delay minimum 4 us (start hold)
- Send SCL low
- Wait ~4 us (Not req'd)

SEND Address:
- Set A6 on SDA
- Delay minimum 250 ns (data setup) 
- Send SCL high
- Delay minimum 5 us (clock hold)
- Send SCL low
- Delay minimum 5 us (data hold)
- ... A5, A4, A3, A2, A1, A0, R/W ...
- Set SDA port to input
- Delay minimum 250 ns (ACK setup)
- Send SCL high
- Delay minimum 5 us (clock hold)
- Send SCL low
